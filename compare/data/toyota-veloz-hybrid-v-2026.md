# Toyota Veloz Hybrid V 2026

## Basic Information
- **Brand:** Toyota
- **Model:** Veloz
- **Trim:** Hybrid V 2026
- **Price:** Rp 308.000.000 (OTR Jakarta Feb 2026)
- **Segment:** Premium MPV
- **Seating Capacity:** 7
- **Colors:** Platinum White Pearl, Silver Metallic, Black Metallic, Dark Red Mica

## Technical Specifications
- **Engine Type:** 2NR-VEX (Full Hybrid EV)
- **Engine Configuration:** 4 Cylinders, Inline 16-valves DOHC with Dual-VVT-i
- **Displacement:** 1496 cc
- **Bore x Stroke:** 72.5 x 90.6 mm
- **Maximum Output (Engine):** 91 PS (67 kW) / 5.500 rpm
- **Maximum Output (Electric Motor):** 80 PS (59 kW)
- **Combined System Output:** 111 PS
- **Maximum Torque (Engine):** 121 Nm / 4.000 - 4.800 rpm
- **Maximum Torque (Electric Motor):** 141 Nm
- **Transmission:** CVT (e-CVT)
- **Drive Train:** FWD (Front Wheel Drive)

### Dimensions
- **Overall Length:** 4475 mm
- **Overall Width:** 1750 mm
- **Overall Height:** 1700 mm
- **Wheelbase:** 2750 mm
- **Minimum Turning Radius:** 5.2 m
- **Minimum Ground Clearance:** 205 mm

### Capacities
- **Fuel Tank:** 43 L
- **Boot Space (Estimated):** 216 L

### Chassis
- **Steering:** Electric Power Steering (EPS)
- **Brakes (Front):** Disc 16"
- **Brakes (Rear):** Disc with EPB (Electronic Parking Brake)
- **Suspension (Front):** MacPherson Strut
- **Suspension (Rear):** Torsion Beam
- **Tire Size:** 205/50 R17 Alloy (Machining)

## Features (V Hybrid Trim)
### Safety & Security
- 2 Airbags (Driver + Passenger)
- ABS + EBD + Brake Assist (BA)
- Vehicle Stability Control (VSC)
- Hill Start Assist (HSA)
- Rear Parking Camera (reverse camera only)
- Rear Clearance Sonar
- Electronic Parking Brake (EPB) with Auto Brake Hold
- **Note:** V variant does NOT have Toyota Safety Sense (TSS)
- **Note:** V variant does NOT have 360° camera (only rear camera)
- **Note:** V variant does NOT have RCTA
- **Note:** TSS, RCTA & 360° camera only on Q variants (Rp 335jt+)

### Exterior
