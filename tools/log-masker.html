Log Masker

Mask sensitive data (emails, IPs, phone numbers, API keys, tokens, credit cards) in your logs. Runs fully offline in your browser.

What gets masked?

  • Email addresses
  • IPv4 and IPv6 addresses
  • Indonesian phone numbers (+62 / 08…)
  • Credentials in key: value form (password, secret, token, api_key, etc.)
  • Long tokens and API keys (sk-, pk-, ak-, api-)
  • Bearer tokens
  • Credit card numbers

Timestamps, dates, and ordinary numbers are preserved. Nothing leaves your browser.
